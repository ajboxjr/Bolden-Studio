About Bolden Studio

Bolden Studio creates, writes, illustrates and self publishes an array of comic books. Created by James Bolden, our comics are entertaining to audiences of all ages seeking adventure, humor and science fiction.

James Bolden is the CEO(Creative Executive Officer) and owner of Bolden Studio. James, a native of Atlanta GA, has had a passion for art from a very young age, selling his first painting at age 9 years old. Over the past 4 years James has developed his talents from simply illustration to writing and publishing. His first products, greeting cards, were created for loved ones, however, after a tremendous requests from family and friends to purchase cards, he began selling greeting cards throughout the community.
